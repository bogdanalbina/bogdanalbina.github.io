Warband Calculator in Python
and deployed as a Flask app

In the famous cross-platform game Diablo Immortal 8 players can band together in a so-called Warband. To get bonus points each can "enshrine" a special weapon. But between 8 weapons, only the strongest 4 determine the bonus attribute. Each weapon can have 2-3 attributes. The purpose is to maximize 5 total bonus attributes. As such, players need to store old items and make complex combinations with available special weapons.

To solve this problem, I created this web application. The warband need to have a simple spreadsheet (.csv file) that keeps track of each players special weapons and their attributes. Once the app read the file, then suggest the best 4 combination that maximize the bonus points, as well as if some weapons can be safely discarded (if their stats are so much lower than anything available). The app is hosted at https://flask-warband-calc.vercel.app/
